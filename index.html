Gluten-Free Lemon Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 250g gluten-free plain flour
  • 2 tsp gluten-free baking powder
  • 1 lemon, zest and juiced
  • 250g icing sugar
  • 1 lemon, juiced
